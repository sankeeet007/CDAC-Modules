All questions are based on the default Emp table.

Q1.Display the oldest employee in the deptno 30.

  select * from emp
  where deptno = 30
  and hiredate = (select min(hiredate) from emp where deptno = 30);

Q2.Display the records of employees who earn salary less than the average salary of job SALESMAN. The records of SALESMAN should be excluded from the
report.

  select ename, sal
  from emp
  where sal < (select avg(sal) from emp where job='Salesman')
  and job <> 'Salesman';

Q3.Display the top earner’s records from the job CLERK.

  select ename, sal as "Top Earner"
  from emp
  where sal = (select max(sal) from emp where job='Clerk');
	
Q4.Display the records of lowest earners who joined in the year 81.

  select * from emp
  where hiredate like '1981%'
  and sal = (select min(sal) from emp where hiredate like '1981%');
	
Q5.Display the records of employees who are earning above the average salary in their own deptno.

  select * from emp e1
  where sal > (
			select avg(sal) 
			from emp e2 
			where e1.deptno = e2.deptno
		);
	
Q6. Display the name, hire date, and salary for all employees who have both the same salary and commission as Scott.
Note: Do not display SCOTT in the result set.

  select ename, hiredate, sal
  from emp
  where (sal, ifnull(comm, -1)) = (
						  select sal, ifnull(comm, -1)
						  from emp
						  where ename = 'SCOTT')
  and ename <> 'SCOTT';

Q7. Display the department names of employees who have their salary greater than Martin’s salary.

   select distinct job
   from emp
   where sal > (select sal from emp where ename = 'MARTIN');

Q8. Display the department name that has the highest salary record.

   select job
   from emp
   where sal = (select max(sal) from emp);

In the TrID column the first 3 characters are country code followed by _, then the next 3 digits are for the transaction serial number followed by _ and finally last two characters are for counter number, i.e. through which counter the transaction has happened.

1) Display all columns along with new column Discounted_Amount as 5% on the
amount for those transactions having quantity sold is above 3 for both the
countries.

	SELECT TrID, PNAME, QTY, amount, amount*0.95 as "Discounted_Amount"
	FROM (SELECT *
			FROM australia as a
			UNION
			SELECT *
			FROM india as i
			)
	AS combined_data  -- combined_data is used as THE ALIAS
	WHERE Qty > 3
	ORDER BY TrID;
